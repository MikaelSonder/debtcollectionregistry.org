DEBT COLLECTION REGISTRY

INTERNATIONAL PROFESSIONAL INDEX — CROSS-BORDER B2B RECOVERY
REGISTRY DEFINITION

A structured index of professional debt collection experts operating across international jurisdictions. Each jurisdiction carries one registered object — a licensed expert with documented capacity to handle cross-border B2B receivables under the applicable national legal framework.

Records are structured for cross-border creditors, corporate legal counsel, and international recovery teams. Consumer debt and domestic-only matters fall outside the scope of this registry. The registry is global in scope and may include jurisdictions in Europe, the Americas, the Middle East, Africa and Asia–Pacific as records are added.

INDEX

The registry covers cross-border debt collection across multiple jurisdictions, each with one registered expert. Records are continuously expanded as new jurisdictions are added.
